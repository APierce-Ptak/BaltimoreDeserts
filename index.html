About Us!

During the HopHacks Hackathon, our team was considering the millions of families in the US who live in areas know as “food deserts”. Food deserts are regions in which affordable access to groceries is limited. In order to raise awareness, we scraped hundreds of webpage to produce a list of grocery stores and food banks across America. Our users will be able to see food desert areas and find food banks that support those who need it most.
